Chocolate Fudge Cake

45 mins Easy Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup coffee
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients, then add wet.
  3. Bake for 30-35 mins.
  4. Let cool and frost.

Classic Vanilla Cake

55 mins Easy Vanilla

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients, then wet.
  3. Add boiling water carefully.
  4. Bake for 30-35 mins.

Carrot Cake

70 mins Medium Vegetable

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup oil
  • 3 eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all ingredients.
  3. Bake for 40-45 mins.
  4. Cool and frost with cream cheese.

Lemon Cake

65 mins Medium Fruit

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Cream butter and sugar.
  3. Add rest of ingredients.
  4. Bake 35-40 mins.

Classic Cheesecake

120 mins Hard No-Bake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Graham cracker crust

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C.
  2. Mix ingredients until smooth.
  3. Pour into crust and bake 60 mins.
  4. Chill for 3+ hours.

Birthday Cake

90 mins Medium Birthday

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp sprinkles

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C.
  2. Cream butter and sugar.
  3. Add remaining ingredients and mix.
  4. Bake for 35-40 mins. Cool and decorate.
